About Us

Eco Activities is a community-driven platform dedicated to promoting environmental events such as tree planting, recycling drives, cleanup campaigns, and awareness programs.

Our mission is to connect volunteers with meaningful ecological activities and help organizers reach motivated participants. Together, we make our community greener, cleaner, and more sustainable.
